### 1.2 Pizza Box FAQs

**Q1: Why are the bundle sizes different for different box sizes?**
A: Bundle sizes are optimized for warehouse handling and customer convenience:
- 10" and 12" boxes: 100 pieces (flat-packed, easier to handle)
- 14" boxes: 50 pieces (larger size, heavier per bundle)

**Q2: Can I order mixed sizes in one delivery?**
A: Yes! You can order any combination of sizes. Just specify quantities for each size in your order.

**Q3: Do pizza boxes come with liners?**
A: Yes, Single Facer Liners are provided free of charge in quantities matching your pizza box order.

**Q4: What's the difference between kraft brown and white boxes?**
A:
- **Kraft Brown**: Natural, eco-friendly appearance; slightly lower cost
- **White**: Premium look; better for custom printing; 5% price premium

**Q5: Can pizza boxes be used in warming ovens?**
A: Our boxes are heat-resistant up to 120°C for short periods (up to 10 minutes). Not recommended for prolonged oven storage.

**Q6: How should I store unused pizza boxes?**
A: Store flat-packed boxes in a cool, dry place away from moisture. Shelf life is 12 months if stored properly.

**Q7: Are your pizza boxes grease-resistant?**
A: Yes, all pizza boxes have a food-grade grease-resistant coating on the interior.

**Q8: Can I order just one bundle to try?**
A: Yes, sample orders are accepted. However, minimum order value of SGD 200 applies for new customers.

### 2.2 Food Container FAQs

**Q1: Are your food containers BPA-free?**
A: Yes, all our food-grade containers are BPA-free and comply with Singapore Food Agency (SFA) standards.

**Q2: Can containers be reused by customers?**
A: While containers are designed for single use, they can be washed and reused several times if handled carefully.

**Q3: Do you offer eco-friendly container options?**
A: Yes! See Section 4 for our bagasse (sugarcane) and PLA (plant-based) container options.

**Q4: What's the difference between PP and PET containers?**
A:
- **PP (Polypropylene)**: More heat-resistant, better for hot foods, microwave-safe
- **PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate)**: Crystal clear, better for cold foods, good visibility

**Q5: Can I mix and match different container types in one order?**
A: Absolutely! Order any combination of containers, lids, and sizes.

**Q6: How many containers fit in one delivery carton?**
A: Varies by container type:
- Single-compartment trays: ~150 pieces per carton
- Multi-compartment trays: ~100 pieces per carton
- Lids: ~200 pieces per carton

## 4. Eco-Friendly Options

### 4.1 Sustainable Product Range

TRIA is committed to environmental sustainability. We offer eco-friendly alternatives across our product range.

---

#### Biodegradable Pizza Boxes
**Material:** FSC-certified cardboard (Forest Stewardship Council)
**Features:**
- 100% recyclable
- Biodegradable within 90 days in commercial composting
- Made from sustainably sourced paper
- Same dimensions as standard boxes

**Pricing:** Standard price (no premium for eco-certification)

---

#### Bagasse (Sugarcane) Containers
**Material:** Sugarcane fiber (agricultural waste byproduct)
**Features:**
- 100% compostable
- Microwave-safe and freezer-safe
- Heat-resistant up to 120°C
- Sturdy and leak-resistant
- Natural white/beige color

**Available Sizes:**
- 6" round bowl
- 8" x 6" rectangular tray
- 9" round plate
- 3-compartment tray

**Pricing:** 10-15% premium over plastic containers

---

#### PLA (Plant-Based Plastic) Containers
**Material:** Polylactic Acid derived from cornstarch
**Features:**
- Looks and feels like traditional plastic
- Compostable in industrial composting facilities
- Crystal clear transparency
- Suitable for cold and room-temperature foods (not hot foods)

**Limitations:**
- Not microwave-safe
- Maximum temperature: 45°C
- Requires industrial composting (not home compostable)

**Pricing:** 12% premium over standard plastic

---

#### Recycled Content Options
**Material:** Post-consumer recycled plastic (rPET)
**Features:**
- 80% recycled content
- Same performance as virgin plastic
- Helps reduce plastic waste
- Fully recyclable after use

**Pricing:** 5% premium over standard plastic

---

### 4.2 Eco-Friendly FAQs

**Q1: What's the difference between biodegradable, compostable, and recyclable?**
A:
- **Biodegradable**: Breaks down naturally over time (timeframe varies)
- **Compostable**: Breaks down in composting conditions within 90-180 days
- **Recyclable**: Can be processed and remade into new products

**Q2: Are eco-friendly options more expensive?**
A: Yes, typically 5-15% more expensive due to specialized materials and manufacturing processes.

**Q3: Can customers dispose of bagasse containers in regular bins?**
A: While compostable, bagasse containers can be disposed of in regular waste. They will break down faster than plastic in landfills.

**Q4: Do eco-friendly containers perform as well as plastic?**
A: Yes! Modern eco-friendly materials match or exceed plastic performance for most applications.

**Q5: How can I communicate to my customers that I use eco-friendly packaging?**
A: We can add "100% Compostable" or eco-certification logos to custom-printed packaging.

**Q6: Are there government incentives for using eco-friendly packaging in Singapore?**
A: Check with NEA (National Environment Agency) for current sustainability grants and programs.
