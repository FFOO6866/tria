## 1. Order Processing Rules

### 1.1 Order Submission
- **Accepted Channels**: WhatsApp, Web Portal, API
- **Business Hours**: Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM SGT
- **After-Hours Orders**: Accepted but processed on next business day
- **Order Format**: Must include outlet name, contact person, contact number, delivery address, and itemized quantities

### 1.2 Order Cutoff Times
- **Standard Orders**:
  - Cutoff time: 2:00 PM SGT
  - Orders placed before 2:00 PM will be processed for next available delivery slot
  - Orders placed after 2:00 PM will be scheduled for the following delivery cycle

- **Urgent Orders**:
  - Marked with keywords: "URGENT", "ASAP", "RUSH", "EMERGENCY", "SAME-DAY"
  - Automatically escalated to Level 2 (Customer Service Representative)
  - Subject to availability and additional rush fees
  - Response time: Within 30 minutes during business hours

### 1.3 Order Modifications
- **Before Processing**: Full modifications allowed (items, quantities, delivery address)
- **After Processing**: Changes require approval from Customer Service Representative
- **Modification Cutoff**: 1 hour before scheduled delivery time
- **Method**: Contact via WhatsApp or call customer service hotline

### 1.4 Order Cancellations
- **Free Cancellation Window**: Up to 24 hours before scheduled delivery
- **Late Cancellation (12-24 hours)**: 25% cancellation fee applied
- **Very Late Cancellation (less than 12 hours)**: 50% cancellation fee applied
- **No-Show/Rejection at Delivery**: Full order amount charged
- **Refund Processing**: 5-7 business days to original payment method

### 1.5 Order Quantity Anomaly Detection
- **Normal Order Range**: Based on customer's historical average Â± 50%
- **Anomaly Triggers**:
  - Quantity exceeds 200% of customer's usual order
  - First-time customer ordering above SGD 1,000
  - Total order value exceeds SGD 5,000
- **Anomaly Handling**:
  - Automatic hold for verification
  - WhatsApp confirmation message sent to customer
  - Level 2 escalation if no response within 2 hours
  - Order released upon customer confirmation

## 2. Pricing and Payment Terms

### 2.1 Pricing Structure
- **Base Currency**: Singapore Dollars (SGD)
- **Tax**: 8% Goods and Services Tax (GST) applied to all orders
- **Pricing Basis**: Per unit as specified in product catalog
- **Price Validity**: Catalog prices valid for current month; subject to monthly review
- **Custom Quotes**: Available for bulk orders (minimum 10,000 units)

### 2.2 Product Pricing Categories
**Pizza Boxes (per piece):**
- 10" Regular Box: SGD 0.45 per piece (bundle: 100 pieces)
- 12" Medium Box: SGD 0.65 per piece (bundle: 100 pieces)
- 14" XL Box: SGD 0.85 per piece (bundle: 50 pieces)

**Food Containers:**
- Single-Compartment Meal Tray: As per current catalog
- Multi-Compartment Meal Tray: As per current catalog
- Container Lids: As per current catalog

**Packaging Materials:**
- Single Facer Liner: Provided free with pizza box orders (quantity matched to boxes)
- Eco-friendly options: Premium pricing (10-15% additional)

### 2.3 Payment Terms
- **New Customers**:
  - First order: Payment before delivery (PayNow, Bank Transfer)
  - Credit account application available after 3 successful orders

- **Established Customers**:
  - Net 30 payment terms (invoice due 30 days from delivery)
  - Early payment discount: 2% if paid within 7 days
  - Credit limit assigned based on order history

- **Accepted Payment Methods**:
  - PayNow (QR/UEN)
  - Bank Transfer (DBS, OCBC, UOB)
  - Corporate Cheque
  - Credit Terms (approved accounts only)

### 2.4 Late Payment Policy
- **Grace Period**: 5 days after due date
- **Late Payment Fee**: 1.5% per month on overdue amount
- **Credit Hold**: Applied after 45 days overdue
- **Collection Process**: Initiated after 60 days overdue

## 3. Delivery Policies and Schedules

### 3.1 Standard Delivery Schedule
- **Delivery Days**: Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday
- **Delivery Windows**:
  - Morning slot: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  - Afternoon slot: 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
- **Delivery Areas**: All Singapore postal codes
- **Minimum Order**: No minimum for established customers; SGD 200 minimum for new customers

### 3.2 Delivery Lead Time
- **Standard Orders**: 2-3 business days from order confirmation
- **Bulk Orders (above 5,000 units)**: 5-7 business days
- **Custom/Printed Items**: 10-14 business days
- **Urgent Orders**: Subject to stock availability and logistics capacity

### 3.3 Delivery Address Requirements
- **Valid Singapore Address**: Full street address with postal code
- **Recipient Information**: Contact person name and mobile number mandatory
- **Access Instructions**: For commercial buildings, provide floor/unit and loading bay information
- **Address Changes**: Must be requested minimum 24 hours before delivery

### 3.4 Delivery Zones and Fees
- **Central Zone**: Free delivery (postal codes 01-45)
- **West Zone**: Free delivery (postal codes 60-73)
- **East Zone**: Free delivery (postal codes 46-59, 81-82)
- **North Zone**: Free delivery (postal codes 75-80)
- **Remote Areas** (Sentosa, Tuas, Offshore): SGD 30 surcharge

### 3.5 Failed Delivery Policy
- **First Attempt**: If recipient unavailable, driver contacts provided mobile number
- **Redelivery Fee**: SGD 50 for same-day reattempt; SGD 30 for next available slot
- **Storage**: Goods stored for maximum 48 hours
- **Uncollected Orders**: After 48 hours, customer charged full amount; goods disposed

## 4. Quality Assurance and Returns

### 4.1 Quality Standards
- **Product Inspection**: All products inspected before dispatch
- **Packaging Integrity**: Products delivered in sealed, undamaged packaging
- **Specifications Compliance**: Products meet stated dimensions and material specifications
- **Hygiene Standards**: Food-grade packaging compliant with Singapore Food Agency (SFA) regulations

### 4.2 Defect Reporting
- **Reporting Window**: Within 24 hours of delivery
- **Reporting Method**: WhatsApp message with photo evidence or email to quality@tria-aibpo.sg
- **Required Information**: Order number, product SKU, defect description, quantity affected, photos
- **Response Time**: Acknowledgment within 2 hours; resolution within 24 hours

### 4.3 Returns and Replacements
- **Defective Products**:
  - Full replacement at no charge
  - Replacement delivered with next scheduled delivery or urgent delivery if critical

- **Wrong Items Delivered**:
  - Correct items dispatched immediately
  - Wrong items collected at no charge

- **Excess Quantity Delivered**:
  - Customer may keep excess or request collection
  - No charge if customer retains excess items

- **Non-Defective Returns**:
  - Not accepted unless due to TRIA error
  - Customer order errors are non-returnable

### 4.4 Compensation Policy
- **Defective Products**: Replacement + 10% discount on next order
- **Late Delivery (beyond promised window)**: 5% discount on current order
- **Wrong Items**: Replacement + SGD 50 service recovery credit
- **Repeated Issues (3+ incidents in 90 days)**: Account review and dedicated account manager assigned

## 5. Data Privacy and PDPA Compliance

### 5.1 Personal Data Collection
**Data Collected:**
- Company/Outlet name
- Contact person name
- Mobile number and WhatsApp number
- Email address
- Delivery address
- Order history and preferences
- Payment information

**Purpose of Collection:**
- Order processing and fulfillment
- Delivery coordination
- Payment processing
- Customer service and support
- Order history analysis for improved service
- Marketing communications (with consent)

### 5.2 PDPA Compliance (Personal Data Protection Act 2012)
- **Consent**: Required before collecting, using, or disclosing personal data
- **Purpose Limitation**: Data used only for stated purposes
- **Notification**: Customers informed of purposes before collection
- **Access**: Customers can request access to their personal data
- **Correction**: Customers can request correction of inaccurate data
- **Retention**: Data retained only as long as necessary for business purposes

### 5.3 Data Security
- **Encryption**: All data transmitted via encrypted channels (TLS 1.3)
- **Access Control**: Role-based access; data accessible only to authorized personnel
- **Audit Trails**: All data access logged and monitored
- **Storage**: Secure cloud storage with redundancy and backup
- **Third-Party Sharing**: Personal data not shared with third parties without consent (except delivery partners for fulfillment)

### 5.4 Customer Rights
- **Right to Access**: Request copy of personal data held (response within 30 days)
- **Right to Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Opt-out of marketing communications anytime
- **Right to Data Portability**: Request data in machine-readable format
- **Right to Deletion**: Request deletion of data (subject to legal/contractual obligations)

**Contact for Privacy Matters:**
- Email: privacy@tria-aibpo.sg
- Data Protection Officer: dpo@tria-aibpo.sg
- Response Time: Within 3 business days
