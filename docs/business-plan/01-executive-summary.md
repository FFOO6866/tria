# TRIA AI Agents-as-a-Service Business Plan

## Executive Summary

**Company**: TRIA AI Solutions
**Model**: B2B SaaS - AI Agents as a Service
**Target Market**: SMBs needing process automation
**Starting Team**: 2 co-founders (technical)
**Development Approach**: AI-assisted development (vibe coding)

---

## Value Proposition

Provide pre-built, configurable AI agents that automate business processes:
- **Order Processing Agent** - Email/WhatsApp order intake, validation, invoicing
- **Customer Service Agent** - FAQ, ticket routing, response generation
- **Data Entry Agent** - Document processing, data extraction, system entry
- **Reporting Agent** - Automated reports, dashboards, alerts

**Key Differentiator**: Self-service configuration - customers configure agents without coding.

---

## Business Model

### Subscription Tiers

| Tier | Monthly Price | Target Customer | Included |
|------|---------------|-----------------|----------|
| **Starter** | $199/mo | Solo/Micro business | 1 agent, 500 tasks/mo |
| **Growth** | $499/mo | Small business | 3 agents, 2,000 tasks/mo |
| **Professional** | $999/mo | Medium business | 5 agents, 5,000 tasks/mo |
| **Enterprise** | $2,499/mo | Larger SMB | Unlimited agents, 15,000 tasks/mo |

### Revenue Streams
1. **Subscription fees** (80% of revenue)
2. **Setup/onboarding fees** - $500-2,000 one-time
3. **Overage charges** - $0.05 per task over limit
4. **Custom integrations** - Project-based pricing

## Funding Requirements

**Bootstrap Phase (Y1)**: $50-80K (founders' savings/sweat equity)
**Growth Phase (Y2-3)**: Optional seed round $200-500K for acceleration
**Scale Phase (Y4-5)**: Cash flow positive, self-funding

---

## Key Success Factors

1. **AI-assisted development** - 2 devs = output of 5-6 traditional devs
2. **Reusable agent templates** - Build once, deploy many
3. **Self-service model** - Minimizes support overhead
4. **Outsourced L2 support** - Scale support without hiring
5. **Low infrastructure costs** - Shared multi-tenant architecture
